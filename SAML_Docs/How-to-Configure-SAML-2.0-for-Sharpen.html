How to Configure SAML 2.0 for Sharpen

This setup might fail without parameter values that are customized for your organization. Please use the Okta Administrator Dashboard to add an application and view the values that are specific for your organization.

  1. Contact the Sharpen Support team and request that they enable SAML 2.0 for your organization.

  2. Include the following with your request:

    • Specify the type of Sharpen instance, one of:

      • Sharpen (app)

      • Sharpen QA (appqa)

      • Sharpen Connect (connect)

      • Sharpen Connect QA (connectqa)

    • Metadata URL: Copy and paste the following:

      Sign into the Okta Admin dashboard to generate this value.

  3. The Sharpen Support team will process your request. After receiving a confirmation email, you can start assigning people to the application.

  4. Done!


Notes:

SP-initiated flows and IdP-initiated flows are supported.

Just In Time (JIT) provisioning is not supported.

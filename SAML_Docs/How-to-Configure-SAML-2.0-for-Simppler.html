How to Configure SAML 2.0 for Simppler

This setup might fail without parameter values that are customized for your organization. Please use the Okta Administrator Dashboard to add an application and view the values that are specific for your organization.

  1. Contact the Simppler Support team at support@simppler.com and request that they enable SAML 2.0 for your account.

  2. Include the following IDP Metadata with your request:

    Sign into the Okta Admin dashboard to generate this value.

  3. The Simppler Support team will process your request and will provide you with their URL in the following format:

    https://www.simppler.com/auth/saml/sso/[customer-uid]

  4. In Okta, select the General tab for the Simppler app, then click Edit.

    • Enter the customer-uid provided by Simppler into the UID field.

    • Click Save.

  5. Done!


Notes:

SP-initiated flows, IDP-initiated flows, and Just In Time (JIT) provisioning are all supported.


For SP-inititated Flows:

  1. Go to https://www.simppler.com/home
